icsp.statustype - Status
  
Label: Status
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: A
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A)ctive, (I)nactive, (L)abor or (S)uperseded
Trigger: 

Indexes:
k-cntnr k-kit


Description:
This field determines the status type of the product. It is used
throughout Trend for all reports, inquiries, and entry functions. Items
appear on the buy list according to their status. Items can be turned
off or on for selling or buying by their status.
 
Content:
A = active
I = inactive
L = labor
S = supercede
