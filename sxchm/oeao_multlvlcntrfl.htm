Label: Override Contractual Pricing
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Override Contractual Pricing to use Multiple Level Price
Trigger: 



Description:
AO option - AO-Product-Multiple Level.
Used specifically with the Multiple Level pricing function.
Contractual Pricing is considered pricing records via Level 1 or Level 2:
Product (Level 1)
Customer/Product (Level 2)
Customer/Prioduct Price Type (Level 2)
Customer/Product Line (Level 2)
Customer/Product Category (Level 2)
Customer/Product Rebate (Level 2)
 
Content:
yes/no
No is the default during install and means that if using multiple level pricing and a level 1 or 2 pricing record is found on the intial system price record, then it will retain the level 1 or 2 pricing record and not look for multiple level records. The initial system price will stick.
Yes means that if a level 1 or 2 pricing record is found initially, then it will continue looking at the multiple level pricing records.
