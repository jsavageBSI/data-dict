icsw.arptype - Authorized Replenishment Path
  
Label: Authorized Replenishment Path
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (V)endor,(W)hse,(C)entral Whses,(K)it,V(M)I or (F)ab VA
Trigger: 

Indexes:
k-arpwhse k-vendprod


Description:
Indicates the source for replenishing this product in this location.
This field defaults first from ICSL (if vendor/line is provided) then
from ICSD. The Authorized Replenishment path methodology was developed
by Dr Gordon Graham and described in his book: Distribution Inventory Management.
 
Content:
V-Vendor Replenish direct from vendor
W-Whse Replenish from another branch
K-Kit Internally produced kit
C-Central Replenish from the Central Whse
M-VMI Replenish from VMI
F-FAB VA Replenish from VA module
