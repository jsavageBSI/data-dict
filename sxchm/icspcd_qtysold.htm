icspcd.qtysold - Sold Quantity
  
Label: Sold Quantity
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Total Quanty Sold. This gets loaded in OEEPI. If an active reservation is
found for the item during order entry, the system will store off that
reservation on the oeelextra.custreserverowpointer field.
Even if the line uses other reservations to fill the line quantity - pulling stock,
the system will store the original active reservation. That is the reservation
that the stock is sold against.
Used to compare to Total Quantity Expected for the contract to see if the
Customer/Ship To meet the requirements for the number of items bought.
