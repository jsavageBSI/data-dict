Description:
For transcd = 11 this show the payment amount which was applied to the invoice.
For transcd = 7, this shows the amount of the check. There may be several transcd 7 records for the same checkno. There will be one instance for each time the amount was applied to an invoice. The aret.amount field will contain the applied amount and paymtamt will always show the amount of the original check.
