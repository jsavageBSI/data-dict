arsg.credlim - Credit Limit
  
Label: Credit Limit
Type: decimal
Format: >>>>>>>>9
Decimals: 0
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Leave at '0' For Unlimited Credit
Trigger: 



Description:
The amount of credit extended by the distributor to the group.
This value is compared against the total balance plus the on order balance
plus the future order balance when determining if an order should be placed
on hold.
The arsg.credlimappty field will control how the credit limit is applied.
If the credit limit is to be controlled by the group, the outstanding balance of
all group members will be compbined and compared against the group credit
limit. The credit limit values on ARSC or ARSS for group members will not be
used.
If the group credit limit is to be used as part of a hierarchy, the outstanding
balance of the individual group member will be compared first against the
group credit limit value. If the order does not go on hold based on the group
credit limit, the outstanding balance of the member will be compagred against
their ARSC or ARSS credit limit.
If the group credit limit is to be bypassed, the outstanding balance of the
individual member will only be compared against their credit limit from either
ARSC or ARSS.
 
Content:
Integer value
Zero = unlimited credit
Non-Zero = credit limit
