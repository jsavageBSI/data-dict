Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?

Description:
If a ship via product restriction is found, and the operator overrode the
restriction in line entry, the system will store the operator who overrode
shipping restriction.
It will be used to flag the line and be able to report or inquiry looking for
overridden restrictions.
