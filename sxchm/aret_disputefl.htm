Description:
This field has a value of y/n. This field designates a transaction as
disputed. Transactions that are disputed are not aged or service
charged. This field is enterable by the user in aret and aremo.
Transactions that are disputed display a 'h' next to the invoice date
of the schedule pay in arit and a 'd' next to the seq number of the
schedule pay in arece. There is a dispute field on the f6 detail record
in aret. This will display yes on the schedule pay records. It will
only display yes on the invoice record if the invoice was flagged as
disputed when entered in aret. If the invoice was not entered as
disputed, but was flagged as disputed in aremo, the invoice detail will
show 'no' in the dispute field. However, the PY will show yes. This is
because the invoice was not disputed at time of entry, and in aremo the
schedule pay is being disputed.
