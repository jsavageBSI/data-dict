oeelb.subtotalfl - Print Subtotal
  
Label: Print Subtotal
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The subtotal flag allows the operator to display a subtotalled net
amount on the invoice and acknowledgement. It is usually used in
conjunction with the printprcfl. If the subtotal flag is set to yes on
line 5, the net amount will be subtotalled for every line above (to the
first line or the a previous subtotal flag) and including line 5.
Typically individual prices will not be printed for lines 1 through 5
and a subtotal after line 5 will print.
 
Content:
yes = print a subtotal after this line prints
no = do not print a subtotal after this line
