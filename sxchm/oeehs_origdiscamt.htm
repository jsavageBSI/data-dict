oeehs.origdiscamt - Orig Disc
  
Label: Orig Disc
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The original discount amount represents the special discount amount for
the price type grouping. It is stored as well as the special discount
amount in OEEHS and used during the proration process in OEEPI. When
prorating to each line, the % of the total net amount the line
represents in the price type group is applied against the original
discount amount. The special discount amount is then lowered by the
amount prorated to each line. The remaining specdiscamt is loaded onto
the last line item for the price type.
 
Content:
oeehs.netamt * oeehs.discpct
