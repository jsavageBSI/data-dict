icsw.replcostfor - Replacement Foreign Cost
  
Label: Replacement Foreign Cost
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Foreign Replacement Cost. This cost also follows the unit and viewing rules
of all costs, but this cost is stored in the currency of the ARP vendor for
the product. It is used only when the currency (on APSV) is not blank.
When cutting PO's for a foreign vendor, either the last foreign cost or the
foreign replacement cost will be used as the default for the line item price
depending upon how the AO - Documents - Purchase Orders - Entry
Defaults: Foreign Cost field is set.
