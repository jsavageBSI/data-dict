arsc.mancustprintty - Print Invoice For
  
Label: Print Invoice For
Type: character
Format: x(2)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
In most cases the Manufacturer Rep Order does not need to print during
invoice processing. The order is used in CSD to determine commission
payment from the vendor and the vendor already has an order in their
system for the customer. the goods are shipped to the customer from the
vendor and the vendor bills the customer.
This option allows the Manufacturer Orders to print differently from the
Floor Plan print option on the OEEPI report. It is setup per vendor since
each vendor may require copies of the orders or the distributor may
want hard copies of the orders
 
Content:
O - Order Customer
I - Invoiced To Customer (floor plan)
N - Neither
B - Both Order Customer and Invoiced to Customer
Blank - Use the Report option setting
