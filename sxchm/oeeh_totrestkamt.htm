Label: Restock Charge

Description:
The total restocking charge on the order. This is accumulated from the
line items each time a restocking charge is applied to a return line
(via the F17 key).
 
Content:
accumulated oeel.restockamt (or multiple the restockamt by the netamt if
the restockfl = no indicating a % charge) (a-oeetlt.i)
