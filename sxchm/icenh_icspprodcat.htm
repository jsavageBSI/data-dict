icenh.icspprodcat - ICSP Category
  
Label: ICSP Category
Type: character
Format: x(4)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Non Stock Inventory Moved to ICSP Product Category
Trigger: 



Description:
When a nonstock product is converted to a stocked product, the product category of the stocked product created will be stored if it is different from the product category of the nonstock product. The product category will be used as a cross reference should someone attempt to use the nonstock product again.
 
Content:
Blank - If the product category was not changed when the nonstock product was converted to a stocked product.
A valid product category - If the product category was changed when the nonstock product was converted to a stocked product, the new product category of the stocked product will be stored.
