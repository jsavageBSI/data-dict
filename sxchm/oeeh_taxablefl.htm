oeeh.taxablefl - Taxable
  
Label: Taxable
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This flag indicates if the customer (order) should be taxed. It defaults
from the customer record but can be overridden.
 
Content:
yes = this order should be taxed (based on the tax status of entered
products)
no = no tax should be charged on this order
