vaesl.qtybasetotfl - Qty Based On Total
  
Label: Qty Based On Total
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field indicates if the total quantity for this line item is based
on the total units produced of the finished product. In most cases, the
line items for the inventory components section would have the total
quantity based on the units produced (recipe format). Although there
may be cases where inventory is to be specified on the ork order, but
the amount of that product isn't based on the units produced.
 
Content:
yes - total quantity is based on the total units produced of the
finished product
no - total quantity is not based on the finished product
