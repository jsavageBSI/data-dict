pdsv.qtybrk - Qty Break
  
Label: Qty Break
Type: integer
Format: zzzzzz9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 8
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: At This Quantity, The Next Level Price or Discount Will be Used
Trigger: 



Description:
Quantity Break. The quantity breaks refer to total quantity, weight, or
cube based on 'buytype'. Note that the figures are per transaction, not
cumulative across transactions.
This field is an array of 8 elements to be used against 9 price or
discount levels. Note that if the transaction meets a given level, the
next level of price or discount is used. Examples:
Ordered 20, level 2 Qty Break = 20. Level 3 price or discount used.
Ordered 18, level 2 Qty Break = 20. Level 2 price or discount used.
