k-arspt k-pricetype


Description:
Must have an ARSPT record setup against a specific customer (ARSC) or Ship To (ARSS) records for the Multiple Level Pricing to function.
 
Content:
The key is the pricetype field. It will point the system to the correct PDSC records within the system that would link to Level 3, Level 4 or Level 6 PDSC records to determine the best price. AO Options control how the best price is found.
