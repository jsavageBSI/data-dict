oeel.prodline - Line
  
Label: Line
Type: character
Format: x(6)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field represents the source product line for the line item. For a
stocked product, it defaults from the ICSL ARP product line
(if entered - regardless of the ARP type). For a non-stock product, it is
the product line as entered on the non-stock screen. For a special order
item, non-stock product, or drop line, this field may duplicate the
oeel.arpprodline.
 
Content:
Validated against ICSL.
