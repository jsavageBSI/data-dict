vaeh.prodcost - Cost
  
Label: Cost
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The product cost of the finished product. If the finished product is
specially costed, this field is represented in the special costing
units. If the product is not speially costed, this field is represented
in the selling unit from the value add transaction banner. The product
cost is calculated by taking the total of all pending amounts (using
either the actual or estimated pending internal amount) plus all the
Work In Process (WIP) amounts, and dividing by the qty ordered.
 
Content:
the product cost of the finished product.
