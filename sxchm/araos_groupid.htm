Description:
Service Charge information can be set up to apply to members of an AR
Master Group.
If the customer whose balances are being rolled in Accounts Receivable
Entry Roll Balances (ARER) is a member of a group, ARER will attempt to
find an ARAOS record for that group. If found, it will be applied to the roll
balances process.
 
Content:
Either blank or a valid, active group identifier set up in Accounts Receivable
Setup Groups (ARSG)
