sasc.wtnsovrfillty - NS Over Receipt Fill
  
Label: NS Over Receipt Fill
Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Blank - Runs Current Functionality, Y)es - Will Over Fill
Trigger: 



Description:
When the recieved amount on a Warehouse Transfer line for a nonstock product is greater than the amount ordered on that line, this field will control whether or not the entire amount received on that WT line is used to update a tied OE or WT line's shipped amount.
 
Content:
Yes - When a nonstock product is over received on a WT line, the receipt amount will automatically update the shipped amount on a tied OE or WT line.
No - When a nonstock product is over received on a WT line, the receipt amount will not automatically update the shipped amount on a tied OE or WT line.
Blank - Runs the functionality as it existed prior to the Nonstock
enhancement added in SX.enterprise 6.0.000
