oeao.multlvlprodfl - Override Product Rebate
  
Label: Override Product Rebate
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Override Product Level Rebate to use Multiple Level Search
Trigger: 



Description:
AO-Products-Multiple Level
Multiple level rebates once setup will apply to all rebate types:
Product (Level 1)
Product Line (Level 2)
Product Category (Level 3)
Product Price Type (Level 4)
Rebate Type (Level 5)
 
Content:
Default is no.
No - means that if a level 1 rebate (Product) is found on the initial system rebate record, then multiple level rebating is not looked at. Stops at the Product level.
Yes - means that if the intiial rebate record is a level 1 product rebate it will continue looking through the multiple level rebate records to determine a best rebate.
