Description:
If the ICSW record is available based on the "Our Part Number" in the
flat file, icsw.prod will be written to the field.
If an ICSC record is available based on an ICSEC Customer's Product or
Interchange, based on the "Our Part Number" in the flat file,
ICSC.catalog will be written to the field.
If none of the above provide a product, then the "Our Part Number" in
the flat file will be written to the field.
