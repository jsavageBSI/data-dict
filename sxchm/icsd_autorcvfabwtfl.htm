icsd.autorcvfabwtfl - Auto WT Rcv
  
Label: Auto WT Rcv
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: WTES/WLEBS - After Ship WT - Auto Rcv WT's For This Fab Whse
Trigger: 



Description:
The new field will sit on the ICSD Required screen next to the WL Location field. It will be labeled ?Auto Rcv?.
The new field will be used to control the Auto WT Receiving that can happen during the shipment of a WT via WTES (Char or GUI) or WLEBS. It is intended for Fabrication warehouses (for VA or KP) that do internal builds. If they use Transfers to move product from one the main distribution warehouse to the fabrication warehouse, then it allows them to auto receive the shipped WT?s by warehouse.
This option cannot be set if the ICSD Whse is a Whse Logistics Whse. That is why it sits next to the WL Location field.
The option is also independant of Direct Orders. The current Auto Receive Direct orders options in WTES will still require the operator to select to do so. WLEBS is always auto receiving for DO orders now.
 
Content:
yes/no - default is no.
