pdsv.prcdisc - Discount
  
Label: Discount
Type: decimal
Format: zz9.999
Decimals: 3
Initial: 0
Extent: 9
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: If "Prices" And "Discounts" Exist, "Discounts" Will be Cleared
Trigger: 



Description:
Discount in percent. This is the discount that will be applied against
the 'vendorcost' field. It should be entered as a percent to be taken
off, for example 10% off of cost.
This is an array of nine elements. The first element will be used until
the first quantity break level is reached. At that point the second
level will be used until the second level is reached, etc.
These levels are used in all programs that calculate the cost of goods
for purchasing. This includes POET, POERR, and POERM.
Note that price multipliers and discount levels are mutually exclusive.
The quantity break points are applied to find EITHER a price multiplier
or a discount, not both. Price multipliers take priority over
discounts - the discount column will be cleared if the price multiplier
column is used.
