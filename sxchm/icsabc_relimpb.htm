Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume, and Customer Criticality.
This table allows for the stratification algorithms to be soft and allow for the users to create their own formulas within the parameters allowed so that they can adjust the stratifications over time as they gain better and better control of their inventory.
 
Content:
This field identifies the percentage for B ranked items used by the Relative Importance calculation.
