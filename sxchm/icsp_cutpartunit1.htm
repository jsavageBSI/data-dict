Description:
SXRF Cut Product Part Unit #1
If the product is set up as a Linear Cut product (icsp.cuttype = L), the
length of a cut piece can be entered as one number (feet) or as two
numbers (feet and inches). This field is used to indicate the unit associated
with the first field.
