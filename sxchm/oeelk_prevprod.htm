oeelk.prevprod - Prev Product
  
Label: Prev Product
Type: character
Format: x(24)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The previous product for the component while in change mode. When the
line item is accessed in change mode, oeelk.shipprod is loaded into
oeelk.prevprod. When the component is updated, the oeelk.shipprod may
be changed. If so, when GO is pressed on the line item, oeelk.prevprod
will be used to back out any ICSW quantities previously updated. This
field should be blank except during change mode of the line item.
 
Content:
Product defined in ICSP.
