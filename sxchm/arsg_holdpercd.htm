arsg.holdpercd - Hold Period
  
Label: Hold Period
Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: If Period Balance Exists, All New Orders Will Be Placed On Hold
Trigger: 



Description:
The hold period indicates an OE order should be placed on hold if the customer
has a balance in the specified period or a later period.
A non-zero hold period indicates that the group hold period will take precedence
over any hold period at the customer level. If the group member has a balance
in the group hold period or later, orders for that group member will go on hold.
A zero hold period indicates that the hold period check will not be applied to
any member of the group. No OE order for a group member will go on hold
based on the hold period
A blank hold period indicates that the group should be bypassed and the hold
period set up on the individual group member's customer record should be
used to determine whether or not the OE order should be placed on hold.
 
Content:
Valid Values: blank, 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
