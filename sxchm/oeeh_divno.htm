oeeh.divno - Division #
  
Label: Division #
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The division for the order. It is used for division GL postings when the
order is invoiced.
 
Content:
This field is loaded from either the division on the customer or the
warehouse, based on the setting of the OEAO flag.
If the AO Use Sales Whse is turned on and the AO Override Division setting
is turned on then the Sales Whse Division is loaded - overriding customer
and order warehouse divisions.
