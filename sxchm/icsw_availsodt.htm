Description:
This date indicates the date that stock became available after a stock
out situation. This date is maintained automatically by all receiving
processes. If there is no stock available at the time that product was
received, then the product is considered to be stock out and the stock
out available date is set to today. This works in conjunction with the
last stock out date and the number of days stock out to make extended
stock out decisions for ICAM. The number of days stock out is also set
by the receiving process.
