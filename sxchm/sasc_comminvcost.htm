sasc.comminvcost - Use Current Commission Cost at Invoicing
  
Label: Use Current Commission Cost at Invoicing
Type: character
Format: x(2)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (D)rop, Tied (S)pecial, (B)oth, or Blank for Neither
Trigger: 



Description:
This field is used to determine whether to honor the ICAOC Commission
Cost during invoicing for drop lines or tied specials.
Blank - no, always use the receiving cost
d - yes, for drop lines
s - yes, for tied specials
b - yes for both drop and tied specials
