oeeh.termsdiscamt - Terms Discount
  
Label: Terms Discount
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The terms discount that can be taken in ARECE by the customer if they
pay by the discount date. It is calculated each time the order is
maintained (in p-oetot.i). It is identified on the ARET record when the
record is created from OEEPI.
 
Content:
If line based terms are used (termslinefl = yes) then:
(termdiscln (accumulated from the netamt or netord from each line
item) - WO discount - special discount - tax) * terms percent
else
(totlineamt - WO discount - special discount = tax) * terms percent
(see p-oetot.i for affect of a negative totlineamt and the ability to
allow credit discounts)
