Description:
The Warehouse Manager quantity shipped controls bin location entry of
the component. If the product is WM controlled, the proper quantities
per bin must be entered prior to invoicing the order. When the wtqtyship
= stkqtyship on a WM controlled component, this line is ready for
invoicing.
