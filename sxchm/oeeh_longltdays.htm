oeeh.longltdays - Lead Time
  
Label: Lead Time
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The longest lead time of all the products on the order - represented in
days. This field is updated from the average lead time of each line item
on all order types. (The average lead time for a non-stock product will
be determined from the ICSL lead time if the non-stock product has a
vendor/line ARP.) This field is used to back up the correct number of
days in order to convert a FO or BR on time based on the promise date of
the order.
NOTE: As of the 5.1 release, the lead time is now enterable on the line
item. It defaults as above, but can be changed.
 
Content:
# of days
