aret.period - Period
  
Label: Period
Type: integer
Format: 9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The Period is used when aging payment transactions (aret.transcd =
11). Periods 1 - 5 are the age buckets. Period 0 is a future
payment. Period 8 is for CODs. Period 9 is for Service Charges. If
the service charge was created in ARER then both the aret.transcd 1
record and the corresponding aret.transcd 11 record will have an
aret.period of 9. If the service charge was created in ARET then the
aret.transcd 1 record will have an aret.period of 0 and the
corresponding aret.transcd 11 record will have an aret.period of 9.
The possible values for aret.period are 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, and 9.
