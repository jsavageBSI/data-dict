Description:
This field is updated on schedule pay (type 11), credit memo (type 6),
and debit memo (type 8) records in the following mannor:
Type 11 - schedule pay:
This is the date that a credit memo or debit memo is applied to this
schedule pay. The date comes in from the invoice date entered in aret
for the credit memo or debit memo. Or, the invoice date of the RM
applied through invoice processing.
Type 06,08 - credit memo/debit memo:
This is the date of the invoice this has been applied to.
Misc credit or unapplied cash amounts applied in arece do NOT update
this field.
