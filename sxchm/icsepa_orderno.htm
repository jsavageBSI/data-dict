Val Exp: ?

Description:
The order number created to bill the customer for adjustments made during
the count update.
The ICSD ?Cost Adjustments Billable? option (ICSD.ADJBILLABLEFL)
controls whether the adjustment should be billed to the customer or not.
If adjustments are to be billable, then any adjustments done will require the
creation of an OE order so that the customer will be billed or credited
depending upon whether the adjustment is negative or positive.
