icsp.msdschgdt - Last MSDS Change
  
Label: Last MSDS Change
Type: date
Format: 99/99/99
Decimals: ?
Initial: ?
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This date is used to record the last time the MSDS information changed
for this product. The field is used in conjunction with the sheet # and
customer information to determine if a new sheet needs to be sent to the
customer.
Note: This date must be changed manually. Chaning ICSEH will not
change all the products associated with that sheet.
