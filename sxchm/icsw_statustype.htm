icsw.statustype - Status
  
Label: Status
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: S
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (D)irect Ship, (O)rder as Needed, (S)tock or (X)-Do not Reorder
Trigger: 



Description:
Status type. This field is used in conjunction with icsw.nonstockty.
When the statustype is set to 'o' for Order As Needed then the
nonstockty field may contain a blank value (to indicate a regular OAN
product) or an 'n' (to indicate an OAN-NS product). When the statustype
field is changed from 'o' to any other value, the nonstockty field must be
cleared.
 
Content:
D = direct ship
O = order as needed
S = stock
X = do not reorder
