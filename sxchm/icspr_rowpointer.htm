Description:
This is the Row Pointer of the Restriction record and is used in the
icsprt.srcrowpointer field (child of the ICSPR record). We use the row pointer
since the parent record could be prod, catelog, prod cat, vendno. prodline,
brand code, alt prod group, etc and no need to duplicate all those fields
within the child table.
One to one match to the specific ICSPR record.
