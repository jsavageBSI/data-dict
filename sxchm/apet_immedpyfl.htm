Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Immediate Pay - If 'Yes', Inoivce Will be Paid on a Cash Basis
Trigger: 



Description:
Immediate Payment option is only available with invoice transactions.
Immediate Payment indicates you will pay the Vendor immediately for the amount of the invoice instead of updating the Vendor current balance. The Invoice (transcd = 0), Manual Payment(transcd =7), and Scheduled Payment (transcd = 3) transactions automatically become inactive. Enterable: The Immediate Payment option is only available with invoice transactions (transcd = 0). This option is not supported for foreign currency transactions. The Vendor's General Ledger bank and discount accounts will automatically be updated instead of the Vendor's General Ledger addon and expense/asset accounts. You are expected to print a check for the amount of the invoice immediately after completing the transaction. This can be done by processing Accounts Payable Check Print (APEC).
By entering in the value of "y" the system will display "yes" into this
field. If "n" is entered into this field the system will display "no".
