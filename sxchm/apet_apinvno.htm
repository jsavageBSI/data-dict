Val Exp: ?

Indexes:
k-apinvno k-inq k-invdt k-novend k-status


Description:
This is the invoice number for the transaction you are processing.
Duplicate active invoices are allowed. Once the invoice becomes
inactive you will be allowed to re-use it. An existing invoice number
is required when entering a credit memo, debit memo or reversal. An
invoice number is not required for a rebate. Allowable values: Up to
15 character positions can be entered into this field. Dashes, slashes,
periods, letters, numbers etc.. are all acceptable characters that can
be entered into this field. Release dependent information: As of
5.0/5.1 transaction types of Miscellaneous Credits and Credit Memos
require invoice numbers and the amounts are stored as negative values.
When going live to 5.0/5.1 the conversion program sequentially assigned all type 5 and 6 records an invoice number.
