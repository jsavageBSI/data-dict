arsg.unearnedty - Unearn Disc
  
Label: Unearn Disc
Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: If "Y", Discounts Can be Entered in Cash Receipts at Any Time
Trigger: 



Description:
This field will control how unearned discounts are applied to members of the group
during processing in Accounts Receivable Entry Cash Receipts (ARECE).
If the group setting for unearned discounts is set to Y - Yes, unearned discounts can
be applied to all members of the group during processing in ARECE
If the group setting for unearned discounts is set to N - No, unearned discounts will
not be allowed for any member of the group during processing in ARECE
If the group setting for unearned discounts is blank, the group will be bypassed and
the unearned discounts setting for the individual group members, found on the
ARSC record, will be used.
 
Content:
Valid Values:
Y, N, blank
