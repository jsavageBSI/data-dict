pv_oeln.restrictovrfl - Restriction Override
  
Label: Restriction Override
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
If a product restriction (ICSPR) record is found - not allowed - the system will
look at the ICSPR record to see if a restriciton can be overriden or not.
- If they are allowed to override the restriction, to complete the sale of the
product, then they must flag the lrestrciton as overridden by setting this flag.
- Even if they are allowe via the ICSPR setting to override the restriction, they
will still be required to have the proper authorization to set the field to yes.
- Via a new auth point for Line Entry.
Used in conjunction with the restrictovrcom field.
