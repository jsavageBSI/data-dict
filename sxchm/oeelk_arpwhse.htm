Description:
The ARP Whse is used for a non-stock or special order component that is
source from a warehouse. If the non-stock is also sourced from a vendor
through the ARP Whse, the arpvendno and arpprodline will also be loaded.
 
Content:
A valid Whse (validated against ICSD). It can not be the same as the
whse on the order.
