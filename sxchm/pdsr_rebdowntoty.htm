pdsr.rebdowntoty - Rebate Down To
  
Label: Rebate Down To
Type: character
Format: x(2)
Decimals: ?
Initial: f
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (F)lat,(A)vg,Las(T),(R)ep,(S)td,R(E)b,or LstF(O)r
Trigger: 



Description:
used when rebate calc type (pdsr.rebcalcty) is "N" for net.
When pdsr.rebdowntoty = "F", SX.e uses "Net Down to a Flat Amt" which is a flat net amount that is compared against a specific price or cost(determined by pdsr.rebcostty) and the difference is the rebate amount.
When pdsr.rebdowntoty &lt;&gt; "F", SX.e uses "Net Down to a Percent of another Cost" which is when a percent of a price or cost (determined by this field) is compared against a specific price or cost (determined by pdsr.rebcostty) and the difference is the rebate amount
 
Content:
F = Flat rate
B = Base price
L = List price
P = PDSC price
A = Average cost
T = Last cost
R = Replacement cost
S = Standard cost
E = Rebate cost
O = Last Foreign cost
C = Actual cost
