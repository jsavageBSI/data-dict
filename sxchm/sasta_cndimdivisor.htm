Transportation Standard Carrier - Extended Screen
Dimensional Weight Divisor. Used with a carrier to determine the minimum
weight per box to use for lighter shipments. It will use the Dimensional
Weight if it is higher than the actual weight of a box for the billable weight
of the package.
