icsp.kitrollty - OE Kit Rollup
  
Label: OE Kit Rollup
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (C)ost, (P)rice, (B)oth or Blank
Trigger: 



Description:
Kit Roll Type. This field is used to control price and cost roll
techniques on build on demand kits only. It can be left blank at any
time and has no affect on any product type other than build on demand
kits.
 
Content:
C=Cost Roll cost of each component to calculate kit cost
P=Price Roll price of each component to calculate kit price
B=Both Roll both cost and price
BLANK Roll neither, cost nor price come from the kit (ICSW/PDSC)
