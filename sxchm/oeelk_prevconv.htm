oeelk.prevconv - Prev Conv
  
Label: Prev Conv
Type: decimal
Format: >>>>>9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The previous conversion factor for the component while in change mode.
When the line item is accessed in change mode, oeelk.conv is loaded into
oeelk.prevconv. When the component is updated, the oeelk.conv may be
changed. If so, when GO is pressed on the line item, oeelk.prevconv will
be used to back out any ICSW quantities previously updated. This field
should be blank except during change mode of the line item.
 
Content:
Conversion factor as defined in SASTT or ICSEU.
