aret.perfisc - Fiscal Period
  
Label: Fiscal Period
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This is the Fiscal Period you are currently posting in. For example
if you started your fiscal year for 1994 on July 1, 1994 for a 12
period year. Then if you made a posting on August 5, 1994, the Fiscal
Period (aret.perfisc) would be 0294. The values of this field can be
from 01YY to 13YY. The field is loaded from OEET, OEEPI, ARET, AREBU,
ARER, AREMP and ARECE. It is set equal to either the global,
g-perfisc, or glet.perfisc which are derived from the GLOA options.
