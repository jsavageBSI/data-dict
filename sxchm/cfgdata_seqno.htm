Description:
Sequence number field. May be an sequence line number from OE, KP, or VA
depending on the value of the ordertype field. This field is only applicable for kit components.
 
Content:
The sequence number defines the sequence of the component. It is part of
the primary key along with the company, order type, order, suffix, and
line number.
