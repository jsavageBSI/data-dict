Description:
The Paid-In-Full Dollar Amount holds any writeoff amount when an
invoice is considered paid-in-full, yet the applied amount does not
equal the payment amount less the discount. If a limit is specified
in SASO0: "Cash Rcpts Writeoff Lmt" (sasoo.arwrtofflim) the
aret.pifamt cannot exceed this limit. Otherwise it must not exceed
the limit specified in ARAO: "Paid in Full Allowance for Cash
Receipts" (sasc.arpiflimit).
While the aret.pifamt for the individual payment (transcd = 11) holds
the writeoff amount for just that transaction, the aret.pifamt for the
originating transaction, (transcd = 0-Invoice or 1-Service Charge or
4-C.O.D.) will accumulate the writeoff amounts for all the associated
payments. For example a total invoice for $100 has two payments
of $50 each, but is paid off with $48 and $49. Each aret for the
payments will have an aret.pifamt of $2 and $1 respectively. But, the
aret for the invoice will have an aret.pifamt of $3.
