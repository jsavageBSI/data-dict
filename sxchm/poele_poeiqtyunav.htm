Help: Quantity Received that is unavailable to sell during POEI
Trigger: 



Description:
This is the quantity unavailable that is reported during POEI. This
quantity is a part of the total quantity received (same as POEI does).
For example if eight units were received, but three were damaged,
poeiqtyrcv will equal eight and poeiqtyunav will equal three, actual
quantity to put away will be five. This field is updated during POEI
F10-Update for an "856i" record.
