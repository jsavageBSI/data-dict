oeao.multlvlprcfl - Multiple Level Pricing
  
Label: Multiple Level Pricing
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Stop Multi-Level Search Once First Multi-Level Hierarchy Found
Trigger: 



Description:
AO-Product-Multiple Level option.
This option is use to determine the use of the multiple level pricing records. It will utilize level 3, 4, or 6 pricing records within the system.
Multiple Price Levels used:
Customer Type/Product (Level 3)
Customer Type/Product Price Type (Level 4)
Customer Type/Product Rebate Type (Level 4)
Customer Type (Level 6)
The option controls if the system will continue looking for 'best' price through the multiple levels, or stop on the first level found and assume that the highest level wins.
 
Content:
Default is no.
No means that the system will continue to look across all levels to find the best price.
Yes means the system will stop on the highest level found and take the best price at that level.
