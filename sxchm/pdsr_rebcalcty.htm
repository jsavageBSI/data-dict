pdsr.rebcalcty - Rebate Calc Type
  
Label: Rebate Calc Type
Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: $
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: ($)Amount, (%)Percent, (N)et, or (M)argin Guaranteed
Trigger: 



Description:
Rebate Calculation Type
 
Content:
$ - Flat Rate - the rebate is a flat dollar amount
% - Percent of Price/Cost - the rebate is a percent of one of the
following prices or costs:
(B)s,(L)st,(P)rc,(A)vg,Ls(T),(R)p,(S)t,R(E)b,LstF(o),Act(C)
n = (when pdsr.rebdownto = "f") - Net Down to a Flat Amt -
a flat net amount is compared against a
specific price or cost and the difference is the rebate amount
n = (when pdsr.rebdownto &lt;&gt; "f") - Net Down to a Percent of another Cost -
a percent of a price or cost is compared against a specific price
or cost and the difference is the rebate amount
m = Guaranteed margin - the margin from the actual price and a specified
cost must be less than the guaranteed margin given in PDSR in order
to qualify for a rebate.
