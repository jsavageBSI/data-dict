icenh.icswprod - ICSW Product
  
Label: ICSW Product
Type: character
Format: x(24)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Non Stock Inventory Moved to ICSW Product
Trigger: 



Description:
When a nonstock product is converted to a stocked product, the name of the stocked product created will be stored if it is different from the name of the nonstock product. The name of the stocked product will be used as a cross reference should someone attempt to use the nonstock product again.
 
Content:
Blank - If the nonstock product was converted to a stocked product but the name was not changed.
A valid ICSW product - If the nonstock product was converted to a stocked product but the name was changed to something other than the original nonstock product name, this field will hold the name of the new stocked product.
