k-credit


Description:
The initials of the credit manager for the group. The credit manager is the
person who is responsible for obtaining credit reports, maintaining and
updating credit information, and approving credit when requested by the
group.
If loaded, the credit notification will be sent to the group credit mananger as
well as the credit manager for the customer in the group placing the order.
If not loaded, only the customer's credit manager will receive the credit
notification.
 
Content:
Must be a valid operator set up in SASO or blank
