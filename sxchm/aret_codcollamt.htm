aret.codcollamt - COD Collected
  
Label: COD Collected
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The purpose of this field is to collect an additional amount from the
customer to go towards a past due amount. The amount will then display in arece when paying the cod. The amount does NOT display in arit. Amount is calculated in pmep, if the customer has an arsa record. Will also be calculated if invoice is cod, and additional amount entered in oees, or oeir. Amount is updated from the oeeh.codcollamt field.
updated in oeepia.p and p-oeepip.i. Read in arececod.p and p-arra.i.
