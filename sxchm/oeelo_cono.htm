Description:
This table is used to build the list of Warehouse for selection of fulfilling
items on a Fulfillment Order. The list is built based on settings in OESF for
rules used and hierarchy of rule use.
The OEELO records are built once a line is created (or the operator changes
the product, unit, or order quantity of the line).
OEELO records are created during OEET, Order Copy, or SXAPI Full Order
Maint.
This is the sourcing record used to show tied to the Tied Fulfillment Orders
once the Tied Orders are created.
