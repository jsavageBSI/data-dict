Description:
The Invoice Date is usually the date on the invoice. It is also used to
calculate the Terms and Discount Due dates. Enterable: You are forced to enter in the date of the invoice. The system date will NOT default into this field. Since you determine which Posting Date and Period you are updating on the journal screen, back dating or future dating the transaction will not have an effect on the period this transaction amount will actually update.
