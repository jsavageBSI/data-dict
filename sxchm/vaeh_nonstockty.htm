vaeh.nonstockty - Non Stock
  
Label: Non Stock
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The nonstock type field indicates whether the finished product on the
value add transaction is a non-stock product or not. A non-stock
product will have the letter "n" in this field. If the finished product
is not a non-stock then it is considered a stock product (ICSP and ICSW
records must exist) and this field will be blank.
 
Content:
blank - stocked product
n - non-stock product
