pv_oeln.restrictovrcom - Restriction Override Comment
  
Label: Restriction Override Comment
Type: character
Format: x(50)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
If a product restriction (ICSPR) record is found - not allowed - the system will
look at the ICSPR record to see if a restriciton can be overriden or not.
If they are allowed to override the restriction and complete the sale of the
product, then they must enter in a 'Comment' as to why they did the override.
Used in conjunction with the restrictovrfl field.
