sasoo.xconocostfl - View Cross Cono Costs
  
Label: View Cross Cono Costs
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: yes
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: If Yes, Can View Costs From Other Companies in ICIA
Trigger: 



Description:
This option will prevent operators from seeing the cost of a product in another
company.
The ICIA warehouse availability screen has the functionality to see the
availability of a product in another company. This option will control the
operator's ability to see the cost of a product in another company.
 
Content:
Yes - The operator can see the cost of a product set up in a different company
No - The operator cannot see the cost of a product set up in a different company
