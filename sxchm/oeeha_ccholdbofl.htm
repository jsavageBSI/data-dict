Help: Hold Credit Card - Zero Ship Tender Order - For BO's
Trigger: 



Description:
This will be a credit card entry made from Tendering. It is like a dead credit card transaction and is used on Back Orders once filled (BO fill) and the order is originally zero shipped. Allows a way for the customer to apply a card to track through the order and not be authorized and applied until the items are ready for shippment (picking). Does not hold up their cards with full order amounts that may not come in for weeks.
