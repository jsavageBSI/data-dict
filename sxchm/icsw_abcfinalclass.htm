icsw.abcfinalclass - ABC Final Class
  
Label: ABC Final Class
Type: character
Format: X(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A), (B) or (C)
Trigger: 



Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer Criticality.
This field stores the final ABC classification of the product in this warehouse. The GMROI, Dollar Sales and Unit Sales are weighted then combined to determine the final classification.
If a customer criticality ranking has been entered, it takes precedence over the value calculated.
 
Content:
The final rankings are determined based on a table developed by Dr. Lawrence.
