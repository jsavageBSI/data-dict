Description:
The ship to location or job for the customer. This can be required entry
or defaulted into OEET. If a ship to exists, pricing can be done by ship
to. Also, the ship to address (rather than the customer address) will
appear on all outgoing documents. The ship to can not be change after
the operator presses &lt;GO&gt; from the banner.
