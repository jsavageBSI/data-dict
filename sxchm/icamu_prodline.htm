icamu.prodline - Product Line

k-vend


Description:
This field represents the source product line for the line item. For a
stocked product, it defaults from the ICSW ARP product line. If the ICSW
product line is blank, the ICAMU product line will be blank. The product
line will be loaded during ICAMU even if the arp type is not a vendor
arp.
 
Content:
Loaded during ICAMM from ICSW.prodline.
