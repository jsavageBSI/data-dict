Description:
The cubes field is the stocking unit cubes for the finished product on
the value add transaction. It is defined with more decimal digits than
the standard ICSP cubes field to allow user to customize where the cubes
came from. In some cases, the actual unit cubes needs to contain more
than our standard 2 decimal digits.
 
Content:
Unit (stocking) cubes.
