Description:
The dimensional Cube Size of the item in the carton. This is used when
comparing the Items to put in the carton. How much of the items will fit
Cubic Feet Dimension.
Height, Width, Length of the carton are measured in Inches, Feet, Yards,
Centimeters or Milimeters. Those dimensions are all convered to inches
and multiplied together and divided by 1728 to get the cubic feet dimension
to be used to see what can fit into the carton dimensionally
 
Content:
Proportioned to the Quantity of the Item in the carton
