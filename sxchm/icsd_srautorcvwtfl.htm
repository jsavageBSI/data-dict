icsd.srautorcvwtfl - SR Auto Rcv WT
  
Label: SR Auto Rcv WT
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Auto Receive WT When the WT is Shipped in Storeroom
Trigger: 



Description:
Storeroom specific option. Used in the SXAPI when the WT is shipped from within
storeroom. The ICSD data is sent to SR via a General Whse Data SXAPI for
use in SR to control the Auto Receiving off the Shipping of the WT. Or if the
new parameter in the SXAPI interface is blank - stating that the WT should
auto receive ('y' or 'n'), then the interface looks at the ICSD for the Ship To
Whse on the WT to see if the system automatically Receives the Shipped
WT.
 
Content:
yes/no - default is no
