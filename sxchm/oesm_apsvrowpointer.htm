Description:
If a vendor is entered, then the rate setup is specific to the vendor entered.
If the vendor field is blank, then the rate setup applies to all vendors
The vendor used to find the rate is pulled from the Manufacturer Order
Manufacturer Customer (oeeh.manarscrowpointer links to arsc.rowpointer)
and the Vendor on the Manufacturer Customer record (arsc.manapsvrowpointer).
