vaesl.wipfl - WIP Flag
  
Label: WIP Flag
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field indicates if the total extended cost of the line item is
reflected in the Work In Process (WIP) General Ledger account. It is
used as a quick method by the Value Add WIP Trial Balance report to
locate the line items that are part of the WIP account.
 
Content:
yes - the line item is in the WIP GL Account
no - the line item is not in WIP
