poele.duedtold - Prev Due
  
Label: Prev Due
Type: date
Format: 99/99/99
Decimals: ?
Initial: ?
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Due Date in POEL before it was possibly changed
Trigger: 



Description:
The POEL.DUEDT field will be written to this field under the following
conditions:
- the line due date in the flat file is different from POEL.DUEDT.
- the line due date in the flat file is blank, but a header due date
was provided in the flat file, therefore the line due date will be
updated with the header due date.
If the line due date will be udpated in POEL, the POEL.DUEDT will be
written to this field before the udpating takes place.
