Description:
Used primarily from StoreRoom to flag an item for Unavailable inspection
during the receiving process. So stock gets set asside as unavailable for
inspection, but this field setting of 'P' allows for the PO replenishment to
include Unavailable Qty in the available stock totals so they do not order
too much stock when there really is stock available.
If the Unavailable Reason is flagged for StoreRoom inspection, then the
unavailable qty with that reason is included in the Qty Available during
Replenishment (via ICSOU Totals and reasons).
