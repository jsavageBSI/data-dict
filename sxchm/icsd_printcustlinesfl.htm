icsd.printcustlinesfl - Print Customer Lines on Invoice
  
Label: Print Customer Lines on Invoice
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: yes
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This flag will indicate if customer inventory lines should be printed on the OE
invoice. Customer inventory lines are identified where oeel.inventoryty = 'C'.
Some Storeroom contracts will require an invoice be given to the customer
for all products issued from the warehouse. Others will not require inventory
which is already owned by the customer to display on the invoice.
 
Content:
Yes - Customer inventory lines should be printed on the invoice
No - Customer inventory lines should not be printed on the invoice
