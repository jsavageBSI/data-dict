icsep.inventoryty - Inventory Type
  
Label: Inventory Type
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field is used to store the whether the adjustment should be made to
customer owned inventory or distributor owned inventory. The field will be
loaded when the count is updated and there is a difference between the
quantity expected and the quantity counted that must be applied.
This field only applies to a count record for a product in a storeroom
mananged warehouse.
 
Content:
C - the adjustment should be made to customer owned inventory
D - the adjustment should be made to distributor owned inventory
blank - the adjustment should be made to distributor owned inventory
