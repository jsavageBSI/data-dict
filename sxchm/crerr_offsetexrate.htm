crerr.offsetexrate - Offset Exchange Rate
  
Label: Offset Exchange Rate
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzz9.9999999
Decimals: 7
Initial: 1
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
For manual matches with an foreign offset bank, this is the exchange rate to convert to the offset bank currency for creating the CRET record.
 
Content:
Defaults from the offset banks currency (SASTC) purchase exchange rate and may be overridden by the user.
For domestic offset banks or if there is no offset bank the offset exchange rate will always be 1.
