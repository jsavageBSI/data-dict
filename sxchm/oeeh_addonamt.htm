oeeh.addonamt - Addon Amt
  
Label: Addon Amt
Type: decimal
Format: zzzz9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 4
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This arrayed field has 4 occurances:
1 - freight in (or addon 1)
2 - freight out (or addon 2)
3 - addon 3
4 - addon 4
The addons default onto the order from the ARSCO addon defaults (the addons are setup and described in SASTO). They are overrideable. The second addon, freight, will be updated from PM if not overridden in OE. The addon amount reflects the dollar or percentage addon (based on the addon type of a $ or %). It does not take into account the minimum or zero addon value. The calculated addon, considering these factors, is stored in addonnet.
As of SX 4.0, the addons are found in the addon table
