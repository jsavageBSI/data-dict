icenh.binloc - Bin Loc
  
Label: Bin Loc
Type: character
Format: xx/xx/xxx/xxx
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 2
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Building / Row / Section / Shelf
Trigger: 



Description:
The bin location field is used to store the location of a nonstock product within the warehouse. The bin location on the ICENH record is also the default location for all future receipts of the nonstock product through either POEI or WTEI.
If there is not a direct location tied to an OE or WT line, then the Default Location off the ICENH record will print on the pick ticket reports.
The default location will also print on the KP and VA pick ticket prints for the non stock item.
