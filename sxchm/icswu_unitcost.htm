icswu.unitcost - Unit Cost
  
Label: Unit Cost
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 25
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Unit cost - This field is updated by ICAM and reflects the unit cost of
the product at the time of the ICAM run.
The field is a table of 25 representing each month from the current
month thru 25 months ago (in reverse order). unitcost[1] is the current
month, unitcost[2] is last month, etc.
