oeel.overridetype - Override Type
  
Label: Override Type
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field contains an indicater to show if a line has a pricing record with
tolerances and whether or not they were overriden.
 
Content:
A - The line (before a manual price change) had a pricing record with
tolerances. The system price was accepted.
I - The line (before a manual price change) had a pricing record with
tolerances. The system price was override within those tolerances.
O - The line (before a manual price change) had a pricing record with
tolerances. The system price was override outside of those tolerances.
