Description:
A modifier can have a specific rank type attached to it. The type can be specific to the ICSW or ICSR setups or specific to Non Stocks.
The rank types can be:
CR - Company Rank (ICSR/ICSW)
WR - Whse Rank (ICSR/ICSW)
CL - Classification (1-13 in ICSW)
AC - ABC Stratification (ICSW)
A Non Stock percent can be applied to any modifier setup and will apply to the non stock items - no ICSW found.
