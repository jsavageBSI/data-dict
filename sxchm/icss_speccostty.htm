icss.speccostty - Special Price/Cost
  
Label: Special Price/Cost
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (Y)es, (T)housand Costing, (H)undred Costing
Trigger: 



Description:
Special Price Cost Type. If not using a special price/cost unit for
this item, this field should be left blank. If using a special
price/cost unit where the price and cost are in the SAME unit (the usual
case), this should by 'Y'. The other values are used only in special
cases where the special pricing unit does not match the special costing
unit.
 
Content:
Y = A Special Pricing/Costing Unit will be used
H = A Special Pricing Unit will be used, costing will be per
hundred pricing units
T = A Special Pricing Unit will be used, costing will be per
thousand pricing units.
&lt;BLANK&gt; No Special Pricing/Costing Unit
Example of 'H':
Price per board foot, cost per hundred board foot
