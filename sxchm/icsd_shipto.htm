Description:
OE orders placed in this warehouse can only be entered for the customer
and ship to combination entered. Entering a customer number and ship to
allows for the setting up the warehouse as storing products for that one
customer - ship to combination.
If no customer number or customer number / ship to is entered, all customers
can purchase from this warehouse.

A valid ARSS ship to for the customer entered
