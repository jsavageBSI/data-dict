vaesl.laborflatrtfl - Labor Flat Rate Flag
  
Label: Labor Flat Rate Flag
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This is the labor flat rate flag. If the line item is contained on an
External processing section, then the line items must be labor products.
The charge associated with this line item can be considered a flat rate
(doesn't matter how much of the finished product is produced), or not
a flat rate (extended cost of the line item is based on the units
produced).
 
Content:
yes - flat rate charge
no - not a flat rate
