Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field is used to indicate problems or warnings that occured during
the posting of physical or cycle count adjustments. They are used in
ICEJ to provide warnings to the user or reviewer of the postings made to
perpetual inventory.
 
Content:
A=Cost Exception Indicates a posting that exceeds the ICAO cost
reporting threshold based on the net amount of
the posting.
N=Negative Indicates that the quantity available (not on-hand)
was driven negative by this posting.
B=Both Indicates that both of the above situations occured.
&lt;BLANK&gt; Neither of the above conditions occured.
