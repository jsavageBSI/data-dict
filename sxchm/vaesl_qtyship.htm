vaesl.qtyship - Qty Ship
  
Label: Qty Ship
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This is the total quantity shipped for this line item. For a stocked
product, it is based on the ICSW availability. For a non-stock product,
it is the quantity that has been acquired from a vendor or another
warehouse and has been filled for this line item. The quantity shipped
is related to the line item's unit of measure.
 
Content:
total qty shipped of the line item
