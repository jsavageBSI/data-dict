icsw.safeallamt - Safety Allowance
  
Label: Safety Allowance
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzz9
Decimals: 0
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Stores the safety allowance amount in stocking units.
When the icsw.safeallty = "Q", this field will be updated directly
from the screen. When the icsw.safeallty = "%", this field will be
calculated based on the icsw.safeallpct field.
 
Content:
The calculation used when the icsw.safeallpct = "%" is:
icsw.safeallamt = icsw.usagerate * (icsw.leadtmavg / 28) *
(icsw.safeallpct / 100)
