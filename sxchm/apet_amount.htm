apet.amount - Amount
  
Label: Amount
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Description: The amount of the invoice.
Enterable:
PO Costing: If the invoice will be costed against a PO it should
include all adonns applicable to this invoice.
Credit Memos: If you are entering a Credit Memo the amount of the
invoice cannot be greater than the amount of the remaining amount of
the invoice.
Reversal: If you are entering a Reversal you will not be able to
enter an amount. The system will default the entire amount of the
invoice you are reversing. Foreign Vendor: If you are using a
foreign vendor the amount should be entered in foreign currency.
Canadian Taxes: If your SASC country flag is 'ca' a window will
appear for entry of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) amount and
Provincial Services Tax (PST). These amounts will be deducted from
the invoice amount and posted to the account codes set up for this
Vendor in APSV.
Allowable values: This field can up to 12 integers including 2 decimal
positions. The field must be greater than zero.
 
Content:
Release dependent information: Miscellaneous Credits and
Credit Memos are stored as negative values.
