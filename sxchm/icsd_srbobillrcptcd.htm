icsd.srbobillrcptcd - BO Billing
  
Label: BO Billing
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: d
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Bill On Receipt BO Billing - C)ustomer or D)istributor
Trigger: 



Description:
Controls the Inventory Type on the OE Line when a line ordered short
from within Storeroom. The SXAPI interface will split the lines if the quantity
is short. For Bill On Receipt items, if the new option is set to Customer, then
the inventory type field is 'c', otherwise it remains as originally coded as 'd'
for distributor.
By allowing the type to be 'c' on the back ordered line, it controls BO Fill not
filling the back orders since the line type is 'c' and Bill On Receipt items are
initially received as 'd'istributor stock. Thus, the auto generated OE Invoice
for the Bill On Receipt items is for the 'full receipt'.
 
Content:
'c' - customer
'd' - distibutor (default)
