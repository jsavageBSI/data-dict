Description:
A link back to the document for which taxes were calculated.
From AP Costing, this is the rowpointer from the AP invoice record
(apet.rowpointer)
 
Content:
A globally unique identifier (GUID). It is stored as a 36-character string value
consisting of 32 hexadecimal digits (0 through 9 and A through F) and 4
hyphens formatted as follows (where X is a hexadecimal digit):
XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX.
