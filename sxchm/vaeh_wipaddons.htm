Description:
This is the accumulation of all the addon amounts that are considered in
a Work In Process (WIP) status, versus pending. The value add
transaction totals are reflected in the WIP status once a completion
activity is performed. A completion activity will move that portion of
the totals from the "pending" fields to the "WIP" fields which reflects
General Ledger updating that has occured in the WIP GL Accounts. The
total value of the finished product always reflects the totals of both
the pending amounts and the WIP amounts.
