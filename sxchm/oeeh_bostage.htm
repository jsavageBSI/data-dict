oeeh.bostage - BO Stage
  
Label: BO Stage
Type: integer
Format: 9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 1
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 1=Entry, 2=Picking, 3=Shipping Feedback, or 4=Invoicing
Trigger: 



Description:
The stage in which the order should be back ordered. It is loaded from
the OEAOB screen when the order is entered. If the OEAOB option is
changed, open orders are not changed.
 
Content:
1 = during entry
2 = when picked
3 = when shipped (f8 on OEES)
4 = when invoiced
