pderv.sourcepros - sourcepros
  
Label: sourcepros
Type: character
Format: x(6)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field is loaded to track the source of creation. It is a
combination of the operator initials formatted to 4 places plus a "-"
plus an "e" if the record is created in entry or a "d" if the record is
created through EDI. Once the field is loaded on initial create, it
is not altered anywhere.
