icsl.rrarunitrnd - RRAR < 1/2 Unit Rounding
  
Label: RRAR < 1/2 Unit Rounding
Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Blank(No Rounding), (U)p to 1 Unit
Trigger: 



Description:
Can have 2 values: Blank or "U".
When it is "U" and the order quantity is less than 1/2 a standard pack,then POERR will round the order quantity UP to 1 standard pack.
If it is blank then no rounding occurs(i.e. still processes as did before t26436).
