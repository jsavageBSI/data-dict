Description:
This field is an audit trail of where this record has been updated from.
On initial creation the field is loaded to an "e" out of PDER or a "d"
out of EDI. Any record that had a "d" loaded initially from EDI that is
altered in PDER will have this field changed to an "m" to indicate that
the EDI transmitted information was manually overwritten.
