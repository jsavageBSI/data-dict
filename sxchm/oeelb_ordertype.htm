oeelb.ordertype - PO/WT/WO #
  
Label: PO/WT/WO #
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: "P" for Accumulative PO or "T" for Transfer In or Blank
Trigger: 



Description:
The order type of a line tie. This field indicates the source for
filling the line item (a PO, WT, or work order).
 
Content:
p - PO
t - Transfer
w - Work Order
blank - from stock (not tied to a PO/WT/work order)
