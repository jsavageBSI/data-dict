icsp.sellmult - Round By
  
Label: Round By
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The Selling Multiplier is used to ensure a standard selling quantity in
Order Entry. If entered, the product can be sold ONLY in multiples of
this number. For example, pipe might be stocked by the foot even though
it comes in 20 foot lengths. To ensure that even lengths are always
sold, set the selling multiplier to '20'. Order entry will default to
20 and will round up any entered quantity to the next even multiplier.
This field is used in Order Entry and in the pricing inquiries OEIP and
ICIP.
