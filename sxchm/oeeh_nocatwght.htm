Description:
The number of line items for which catch weight quantities need to be
entered. This field is automatically updated as line items are
maintained. For each line that has a quantity shipped &gt; 0 and the charge
quantity has not yet be entered, this field will accumulate a value of
1. An order can not be invoiced until the charge quantity has been
entered on all lines that require entry of a charge quantity.
NOTE: We no longer allow entry of a "c" (catch weight) special costing
type in ICSP. This field is currently not used. We do have plans to
reinstate charge quantity functionality in OE in the future.
