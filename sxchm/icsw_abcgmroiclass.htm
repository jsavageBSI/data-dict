icsw.abcgmroiclass - ABC GMROI Class
  
Label: ABC GMROI Class
Type: character
Format: X(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A), (B) or (C)
Trigger: 



Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer Criticality.
This field stores the ranking for Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI) of a product. The GMROI is calculated by dividing the gross margin for a product by the average inventory cost of that product.
The ranking is determined based on the GMROI value.
 
Content:
The GMROI ranking is determined as follows:
A - GMROI is 200% or more
B - GMROI is between 80% and 200%
or
GMROI is between 20% and 80% AND the sales of the product account
for more than 3% of the total branch sales
C - Remaining items
X - Products with zero inventory and a positive gross margin
Y - Products with zero inventory and a negative gross margin
