Description:
The ARP product line this product is sourced from. This field is only
loaded for non-stock products, special order items, and drop ship lines.
For a non-stock product, this field is enterable on the non-stock
screen. For a stocked product, this field defaults from the ARP product
line in ICSW. This field is used for special and non-stock lines that
need to be picked up on a RRAR.
