oeehs.discpct - Discount %
  
Label: Discount %
Type: decimal
Format: >>9.999
Decimals: 3
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The discount percent that qualified for the price type group. This is
loaded based on the PDSS quantity break the line items qualify for. It
is based on the accumulated order quantity or net for the lines on the
-00 order. When a back order is created, the discount percent from the
-00 OEEHS will be copied to the back order.
