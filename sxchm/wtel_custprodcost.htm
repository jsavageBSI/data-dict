wtel.custprodcost - Customer Cost
  
Label: Customer Cost
Type: decimal
Format: >>>>>>9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Customer Cost When the Customer Inventory is Shipped
Trigger: 



Description:
Loads in WTES during the Final Update. If the Shipping Whse is Storeroom
managed and there is stock available in that warehouse that is Customer
Owned, then the new field is loaded with the Customer Cost off the ICSW
record of the shipping whse. There is a setting at the Whse level to determine
which cost to use in ICSW - Fixed Customer Cost or Average Customer Cost.
The Cost loaded on the WT Line is used in WTEI for the Customer Owned
quantity that is shipped to the receiving warehouse for ICETC updates,
Customer Average Cost calculations, etc.
 
Content:
Only loads if there is Customer Owned inverntory to transfer in WTES.
