oeel.catwtfl - Catch Wt
  
Label: Catch Wt
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Indicates if the charge quantity needs to be entered on the line item for a catch weight product. If the quantity shipped is greater than 0 and the charge quantity = 0, this flag will be yes. Otherwise it will be no. (NOTE: we do not allow enter of catch weight products in ICSP as of V3.0. Keep all logic that references charge quantity as we may reinstitute catch weight in a new form in the future.)
 
Content:
yes = quantity shipped &gt; 0 and charge quantity = 0
no = not a catch weight product or the chrage quantity has been entered on a shipped line. The charge quantity is enterable in OEES.
oeeh.nocatwght is accumulated from each line with a catwtfl = yes
