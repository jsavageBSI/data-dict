Description:
The product line or warehouse this product is sourced from. This
field is only loaded for non-stock products, special order items,
and drop ship lines. For a non-stock product, this field is
enterable on the non-stock screen. If the ARP whse on the non-stock
screen is entered, the arpprodline will contain the whse and the arpvendno
will be 0. If the ARP whse is not entered on the non-stock screen, the
arpprodline will contain the product line entered on the non-stock screen.
For a stocked product, this field defaults from either the ARP whse
(if the ARP is a whse) or the ARP product line set in ICSW. This field is
used for special and non-stock lines that need to be picked up on a RRAR.

Validated against ICSL.
