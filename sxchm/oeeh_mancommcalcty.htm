Description:
A distributor may elect to pay a portion of the Manufacturer Commisison
paid to the distributor with the sales reps on the Manufacturer Rep Orders.
During SMRC when the commission is determined for the sales reps of the
orders, this option will determine on the Man rep Orders how the rate is
applied. Against the Sales Amount of the lines (netamt) or the Commission
Amount paid to the distributor from the vendor.
The value is pulled in from the ARSC Manufacturer Vendor customer setting,
but loaded on the order for use in SMRC and commission to the sales reps
 
Content:
Blank - Earned Commission Amount
S - Sales Amount
