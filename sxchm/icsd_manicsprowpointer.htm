Description:
When a Manufacturer Total Sales Order is created in OEET, it means the
vendor sent a commission check to the distributor and there was no actual
Man rep Order created in the ssystem that mirrored the vendors order to the
customer.
All the distributor knows is the Commission amount and the Sales Amount of
the order. They do not know the items on the order. If they do know the
items on the order, then they would not use a Total Sales order
When using the total sales order, this is the default item used that is
created on the order. They will enter in the Commission Amount and
the Total Sales amount of the order when the order is created and those
amounts load into the ICSD Default item automatically when the order
is created.
Saves a step of the operator putting an item on the order. the item is needed
fro SMRC Sales rep commission processing and tracking of sales.
