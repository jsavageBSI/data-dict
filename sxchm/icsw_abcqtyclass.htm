icsw.abcqtyclass - ABC Quantity Class
  
Label: ABC Quantity Class
Type: character
Format: X(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A), (B) or (C)
Trigger: 



Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer Criticality.
This field stores the ranking for unit sales volume of a product. The
ranking is determined based on the ratio of unit sales of this product
to the total unit sales in the branch.
 
Content:
The Quantity ranking is determined as follows:
A - Items contributing 80% of total sales unit quantity for the branch
B - Items contributing the next 10% of the total unit quantity for the
branch
C - Remaining items
