Description:
'Auto Assign' means the system will auto create cartons from the picking
runs - OEEPP and WTEP. It also allowed carton creation from ICECN.
'Manually Assign' means there is no auto creation of cartons from OEEPP
or WTEP, but the can create cartons based on the orders selected
from ICECN
Blank means the current warehouse does not allow Cartonization
 
Content:
Auto Assign Order Entry Orders (AO)
Auto Assign Warehouse Transfers (AT)
Auto Assign Orders and Transfers (AB)
Manually Assign Order Entry Orders (MO)
Manually Assign Warehouse Transfers (MT)
Manually Assign Orders and Transfers (MB)
No Cartonization (Blank)
