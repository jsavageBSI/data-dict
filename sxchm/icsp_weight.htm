icsp.weight - Weight
  
Label: Weight
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Weight is expressed per stocking unit. It is used in Trend in several
areas:
1) Picking documents print total weight sum(item * qty) at bottom
2) Purchasing. In ICSL, minimum and target information can be
expressed in weight. If so, all buying decisions will be
compared to target weight, discounts will be expressed in
weights met, and review cycle information (APSP) will be
stored as total weight.
