icenh.icspaltprodgrp - ICSP Alternate Product Group
  
Label: ICSP Alternate Product Group
Type: character
Format: x(8)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Non Stock Inventory Moved to ICSP Alternate Product Group
Trigger: 



Description:
Added with JIRA MSF-197463/SXWEB-10969. When a non-stock product is converted to a stocked product, the alternate product group of the stocked product created will be stored if it is different from the alternate product group of the non-stock product. The alternate product group will be used as a cross reference should someone attempt to use the non-stock product again.
 
Content:
Blank - If the alternate product group was not changed when the non-stock product was converted to a stocked product.
A valid alternate product group - If the alternate product group was changed when the non-stock product was converted to a stocked product, the new alternate product group of the stocked product will be stored.
