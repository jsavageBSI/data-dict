icet.divno - Division #
  
Label: Division #
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The division number of the warehouse affected by the transaction. In
most situations, this division will be the same as the ICSD division.
You can, however, choose to have the division number loaded from the
customer record. In this case ICET records that are associated with
customer transactions will be loaded with the division number from the
customer record. All other cases and all other settings will produce a
division number matching that of ICSD. This is the recommended use of
the the field.
