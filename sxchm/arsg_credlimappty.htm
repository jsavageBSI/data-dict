Type: character
Format: x
Decimals: ?
Initial: C
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Indicates How the Group Credit Limit Should Be Applied
Trigger: 



Description:
Determines how the group credit limit value should be applied with regards
to the members of the group.
Apply Group Limit - If the credit limit is to be controlled by the group, the
outstanding balance of all group members will be compbined and compared
against the group credit limit. The credit limit values on ARSC or ARSS for
group members will not be used.
Group In Hierarchy - If the group credit limit is to be used as part of a hierarchy,
the outstanding balance of the individual group member will be compared first
against the group credit limit value. If the order does not go on hold based on
the group credit limit, the outstanding balance of the member will be compared
against their ARSC or ARSS credit limit.
Bypass To Customer - If the group credit limit is to be bypassed, the outstanding
balance of the individual member will only be compared against their credit limit
from either ARSC or ARSS.
 
Content:
G - apply Group credit limit
H - apply group credtit limit in a Hiearchy with customer or ship to
C - bypass the group credit limit and apply the Customer or ship to limit
