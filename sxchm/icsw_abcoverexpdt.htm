Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer Criticality.
This field stores the ranking for the customer criticality of a product. The ranking is determined by the branch manager; it is not calculated. It takes into account the significance of the product to the customer and the significance of the customer to the distributor.
The customer criticality ranking will take precedence over all other ABC classification rankings when determining the final ABC classification.
This field will allow users to set an expiration date on this customer assigned ABC value.
 
Content:
For most products, the ABC Customer classification will be left blank.
In the case that it is used this field should be used to assign an expiration date that the ABC Customer classification will expire.
This field and the ABC Customer classification will be cleared when an ABC stratification is performed and this fields date value has been passed. The final ABC stratification value will then be based on the calculated value.
