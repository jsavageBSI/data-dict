oeeh.fpcustno - Invoice To
  
Label: Invoice To
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzzzzz9
Decimals: 0
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The customer that is floor planning the order. This is the customer that
will be invoiced during invoice processing, but the customer (custno)
will be shipped the goods.
 
Content:
If the ARSC Invoice To field on the customer record is not 0, that
customer will default into the floor plan customer field. The operator
will have the opportunity to override the defaulted floor plan customer.
