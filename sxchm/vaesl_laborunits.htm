vaesl.laborunits - Labor Units
  
Label: Labor Units
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
If the line item is not a flat rate charge (vaesl.laborflatrtfl = no)
then the labor type indicates what the charge is based (Qty, Weight,
or Cube). The labor units contain eight the qty, weight, or cube of the
finished product that the charge will be based. Example:
Labor Chrg Labor Type Labor Units Units Finished product Total
$50 Q 100 2000 $1000
$250 W 100 500 $1250
$75 C 50 150 $225
 
Content:
Labor units, based on the labor type, it will either be a qty
(stocking), weight, or cube.
