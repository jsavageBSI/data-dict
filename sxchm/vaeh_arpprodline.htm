Description:
The ARP product line for the finished product on the value add
transaction. This field is only loaded for non-stock products. For a
non-stock product, this field is enterable on the non-stock screen. For
a stocked product, this field defaults from the ARP product line in
ICSW. This field is used at the VA header level to further desribe the
non-stock product.
