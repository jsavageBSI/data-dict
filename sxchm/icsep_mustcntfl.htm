Description:
This field indicates that the product was added to the count run as a must
count item.
A product can be flagged as 'must count' in either Order Entry
Shipping Feedback (OEES) or Warehouse Transfer Entry Shipping (WTES)
when a quantity discrepancy occurs. Other processes (OEEPI for example)
will set a product to be 'must count' automatically based on odd stock situations.
