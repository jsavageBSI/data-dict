Description:
The exception type. This field is coded to describe each invoice
processing exception.
a10-Order not cancelled - Customer or bank invalid
a11-Order not finished
a12-Back orders need to be created
a13-Orders on hold or not approved
a14-Serial/lot #'s not full allocated/over allocated
a15-Charge quantity not entered for a catch weight item
a16/17-Inbound/Outbound freight amount not entered
a18-Ship complete/Tag and hold orders not completely filled
a19-AR Invoice #/Suffix # already exists
a1a-Customer or ship to not found
a1b-Order in use by another operator
a1c-Down payment amount is &gt; invoice amount
a1d-Order not ready for invoicing - no lines or nothing shipped/returned
a1e-A COD CR debit is not allowed
a1f-Invoice to customer is not set up in ARSC
a1g-Your location may not modify the document at this stage
