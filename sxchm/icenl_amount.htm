icenl.amount - Amount
  
Label: Amount
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzzzz9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Amount posted to GL as a result of this record. This record should
always represent the total dollars regardless of the quantity. It will
always be positive if the qty field is not zero. ( The qty field will
be negative indicating a decrease of inventory.) If, however, the qty
field is zero and a negative write off occurred, the amount field will
be represented as negative.
