icsw.safeallpct - Safety Allowance
  
Label: Safety Allowance
Type: decimal
Format: zz9
Decimals: 0
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Stores safety allowance as a percent
When the icsw.safeallty = "%", this field is directly updated from the
screen. When the icsw.safeallty = "Q", this field will be calculated
from the icsw.safeallamt field.
 
Content:
The calculation used for this field when icsw.safeallty = "Q" is:
icsw.safeallpct = (icsw.safeallamt * 100) /
(icsw.usagerate * (icsw.leadtmavg / 28))
