icsw.regrindfl - Regrind Flag
  
Label: Regrind Flag
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field indicates if the product, when consumed, is able to be reground,
sharpened, repaired or refurbished into a usable product
 
Content:
Yes - Product can be reground for continued use
No - Product cannot be reground for continued use
