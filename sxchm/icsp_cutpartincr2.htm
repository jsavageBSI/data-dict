icsp.cutpartincr2 - 2nd Part Increment
  
Label: 2nd Part Increment
Type: integer
Format: >>>9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: Enter the 2nd Part Increment - How Many 2nd Part Units in Each 1st Part
Trigger: 



Description:
SXRF Cut Product Part Increment #2
If the product is set up as a Linear Cut product (icsp.cuttype = L), the
length of a cut piece can be entered as one number (feet) or as two
numbers (feet and inches). If the length is entered as two numbers, this field
stores the relationship between the two.
For example: 12 inches per foot or 100 centimeters per meter
 
Content:
A numeric value relating the two length fields used to enter dimensional
information.
