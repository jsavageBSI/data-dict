apss.addressoverfl - Address Override
  
Label: Address Override
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
Used in conjuction with AvaTax Address Verification and may be enhanced to include other address verifications.
Yes indicates that the address verification failed but the entered address should be saved as is (perhaps it is a new address not yet in the USPS database).
For AvaTax, yes will automatically replace address 2 with "General Delivery" when taxes are calculated.
