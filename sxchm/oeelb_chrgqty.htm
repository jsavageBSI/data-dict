oeelb.chrgqty - Charge Qty
  
Label: Charge Qty
Type: decimal
Format: >>>>>>9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The charge quantity for a catch weight product. This field is enterable
on the extended screen in OEES. It is used to reprice/cost the line
items based on the weight of the line. (NOTE: we now longer allow entry
of a catch weight product in ICSP as of V3.0. Keep all code intact in
case we reestablish this functionality.)
 
Content:
The total quantity to base price/cost from. For instance, if the
customer orders 5 boxes of 10 per box and each box typically has 10 lbs
per box, the charge quantity would generally be close to 50 lbs. If 48
were entered in the charge quantity of the line, the customer would only
be charged for 48 pounds even though they would receive 5 boxes (and
inventory would be relieved by 5 boxes).
