Description:
Product Category. This field is required and validated against 'C'
entries in SASTT. This is usually the primary G/L breakout for
inventory, sales and cost of goods sold accounts. Sales manager is
usually also stored by prodcat. Many of the inventory and sales manager
reports and inquiries can also be directed by prodcat. Two notes:
1) prodcat is NOT the primary control field for ordering. Those
fields are in ICSW.
2) prodcat should NOT be changed lightly. If posting SM or GL by
prodcat any changes to an established product will have to be
accompanied by manual changes to SM and/or GL balances. Changing
the prodcat is an easy way to throw inventory out of balance.
