Description:
Indicates to what level of jurisdiction a district tax must be remitted.
 
Content:
6 - District tax remitted to a federal level jurisdiction
7 - District tax remitted to a state level jurisdiction
8 - District tax remitted to a county level jurisdiction
9 - District tax remitted to a city level jurisdiction
