oeehb.stagecd - Stg
  
Label: Stg
Type: integer
Format: 9
Decimals: ?
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (1)-Order, (2)-Pick, (3)-Ship, (4)-Invoice, (5)-Pay, (9)-Cancel
Trigger: 



Description:
The stage of the order.
 
Content:
0 = entered (used for FO, BL, ST, and QU order types)
1 = ordered
2 = picked (orders picked in OEEPP or a suspended CS order)
3 = shipped (set in OEES from F8 shipping, PM, or upon entry of a CS
or CR order)
4 = invoices (set in OEEPI)
5 = paid (set from ARECE upon full payment)
9 = cancelled (upon order cancel from the header or from OEEPC/OEEPI)
