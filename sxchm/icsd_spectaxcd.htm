Description:
Special Tax Code - If Taxware is currently being run in a company the value can be set to UEZ or blank.
Urban Enterprise Zones (UEZ) are intended to encourage development in blighted neighborhoods through tax and regulatory relief to entrepreneurs and investors who launch businesses in the area. A UEZ is intended to foster an economic climate that revitalizes the designated area and stimulates growth by encouraging business to develop and create jobs.
In Urban Enterprise Zones, Order Entry orders may be taxed at a reduced rate.

UEZ or blank. Default=blank.
