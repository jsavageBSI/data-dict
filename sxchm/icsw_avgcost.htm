Description:
Average Cost. This field is maintained by the system and should not be
changed by the user. It is protected by the see cost flag in SASOO.
This field is an integral part of IC balancing. The average cost
represents the average cost of the items in stock. It is updated by all
process affecting inventory quantities. This field is stored either in
stocking units or in 'raw' cost - special price/cost units.
Average cost is updated during receiving by taking the old cost times
the old qty, adding the received quantity times the new cost, and
dividing the sum by the new total quantity:
$10 * 50 = 500
$15 * 10 = 150
== ===
60 650 $650 / 60 = 10.833 each
A similar process is done if items are removed from stock at any cost
other than average. IC Balancing documents explain the entire process.
