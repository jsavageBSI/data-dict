icsp.unitstock - Stocking Units
  
Label: Stocking Units
Type: character
Format: x(4)
Decimals: ?
Initial: each
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The stocking unit is required for every product. It is not validated
against any unit setups and is always assumed to represent a single unit
in all quantity fields for the product. It is normally 'each'. It is
HIGHLY RECOMMENDED that the stocking unit be the smallest possible
selling unit to avoid fractional quantities.
The stocking unit is also used as an automatic default for all other
units for the product. Any unit (other than stocking) that is left
blank will be considered equal to the stocking unit.
The stocking unit is the basis for all quantity transactions in Trend.
As such, it is very important to not change the value of this field in
mid-stream. Careful consideration must be given to the effect on:
1) Quantities and Balances in ICSW
2) Pricing in ICSW, PDS*
3) Outstanding orders, transfers, purchase orders
4) GL Balances
5) History on file
