icsw.abcsalesclass - ABC Sales Class
  
Label: ABC Sales Class
Type: character
Format: X(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A), (B) or (C)
Trigger: 



Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory
classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The
classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment
(GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer
Criticality.
This field stores the ranking for dollar sales volume of a product. The ranking is determined based on the ratio of dollar sales of this product to the total dollar sales in the branch.
 
Content:
The ABC Sales ranking is determined as follows:
A - Items contributing to 80% of the total sales for the branch
B - Items contributing the next 10% of the total sales for the branch
C - Remaining items
