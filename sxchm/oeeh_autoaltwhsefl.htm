Label: Auto Alternate Whse

Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: yes
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
WT Billed Order.
If an OE order is automatically generated from a Whse Transfer based on the following edits, then the
order is just a 'bill' for the customer that owns the warehouse inventory and the
OE order is used to bill them for the transferred stock.
Edits:
Ship To Whse on WT is a Customer Owned whse (icsd.custno ne 0)
Ship From Whse is linked to the customer owned whse as an Alternate Whse
SASA Other License - 'Auto AltLn Split'
ICSD of the Customer Whse is set to Auto Split (icsd.altwhsefillty)
