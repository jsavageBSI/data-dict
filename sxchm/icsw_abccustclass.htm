icsw.abccustclass - ABC Customer Class
  
Label: ABC Customer Class
Type: character
Format: X(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (A), (B) or (C)
Trigger: 



Description:
Dr. Barry Lawrence of Texas A&M University has created a new inventory classification method called the ABC classification of inventory. The classification is determined by Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI), Dollar Sales Volume, Unit Sales Volume and Customer Criticality.
This field stores the ranking for the customer criticality of a product. The ranking is determined by the branch manager; it is notcalculated. It takes into account the significance of the product to the customer and the significance of the customer to the distributor.
The customer criticality ranking will take precedence over all other ABC classification rankings when determining the final ABC classification.
 
Content:
For most products, the ABC Customer classification will be left blank.
This field should be used for products where the branch manager feels the other classification ranking do not accurately reflect how this product relates to business at the branch.
If the branch's top customer is now very interested in purchasing this product, the branch manager can set the class to A so that it is seen as important to stock.
If the branch's bottom customer is very interested in a product, the branch manager may set the class to C because the business of that one customer does not impact the overall sales of the branch and the product should not be stocked.
