poela.exchgrate - Exchange Rate
  
Label: Exchange Rate
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzz9.9999999
Decimals: 7
Initial: 1
Extent: 2
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
This field has an extent of 2. exchgrate[1] is loaded with the vendor
to domestic exchange rate The vendor is the vendor for the invoice. The
vendor exchange rate is loaded from the first pop up window after
entering the vendor for the invoice and the type. This field will
default to 1 and is loaded when dealing with regular addons or discount.
exchgrate[2] also defaults to 1 and is the po to domestic exchange rate.
This field is loaded with the vendor to domestic exchange rate except in
the case of doing third party addons and applying them to a specific
po. In this case the exchange rate window pops up and the exchange
rate entered in this field loads into the exchgrate[2] field.
