oeeh.orderdisp - Disposition
  
Label: Disposition
Type: character
Format: x(1)
Decimals: ?
Initial: 
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: (S)hip Complete, (T)ag & Hold, (W)ill Call, (J)IT or Blank
Trigger: 



Description:
The disposition of the order.
 
Content:
s = ship complete. A ship complete order will never back order. All
lines remain on the -00 suffix until the order is fully filled and
invoiced. A ship complete order will not invoice until all lines are
fully filled.
t = tag and hold. A tag and hold order currently operates exactly like a
ship complete order. It must be completely filled to invoice.
w = will call. This means the customer wants to be called each time any
part of the order is filled. The customer may come pick up the order or
may have it delivered/sent.
j = jit. A just in time order indicates that line delivery dates are
used. A jit order has far reaching impact on picking, shipping, and
invoicing. Please refer to the documentation.
On a ship complete, will call, and tag and hold order, the pick ticket
will operate a little differently. If the staging area is blank, it
will be loaded with all "*". A tag and hold and will call order will not
print the shipping address. A ship complete order that is not complete
will also not print the shipping address.
