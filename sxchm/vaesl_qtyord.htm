Description:
This is the total qty for this line item. It is calculated as follows:
If the quantity based on total flag = yes, then it's the quantity
needed * quantity ordered (vaeh.stkqtyord) of the finished product
If the quantity based on total flag = no, then it's simply the
quantity needed.
The quantity ordered is related to the line item unit of measure.
