oeelb.printpricefl - Print Invoice Price
  
Label: Print Invoice Price
Type: logical
Format: yes/no
Decimals: ?
Initial: no
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The print price flag is an enterable field on the line item extended
screen. This flag indicates if the product price/discount/net should be
printed on the invoice and acknowledgement.
 
Content:
yes = print the price
no = do not print the price
