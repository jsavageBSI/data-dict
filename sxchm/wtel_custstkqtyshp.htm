wtel.custstkqtyshp - Customer Stk Qty
  
Label: Customer Stk Qty
Type: decimal
Format: >>>>>>9.99-
Decimals: 2
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: How Much of the Inventory Shipped is Customer Owned
Trigger: 



Description:
Loads in WTES during the Final Update. If the Shipping Whse is Storeroom
managed and there is stock available in that warehouse that is Customer
Owned, then the new field is loaded with what ever amount of the line's
Stock Qty Shipped amount that it can (full amount, partial amount or none).
The quantity loaded on the WT Line is used in WTEI for the Customer Owned
quantity that is shipped to the receiving warehouse for ICETC updates,
Customer Average Cost calculations, etc.
It is a sub-set of the wtel.stkqtyship field - meaning this old field is still the
full shipped quantity for the line item, and the new customer field reflects
how much of the line quantity is customer owned when shipped.
 
Content:
Customer Owned inventory that is shipped on the WT.
