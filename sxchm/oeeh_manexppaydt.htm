Description:
When is the Vendor expected to make payment of the commission for this
order.
Compared against the actual Paid date of the order.
Initially calculated off the Request Ship Date and the Manufacturer Customer
terms setting (Req Ship Date + Due Date)
Once the Actual Manufacturer Shipment Date (oeeh.manactshipdt) is entered
by the operator, then the date is recalcuated based on the Terms of the
Manufacture Sales Customer (Terms Due Date + Expected Ship Date)
Informational Only. In place for Birst Reporting
 
Content:
Terms Due Date + (Req Ship Dt or Actual Manufacture Shipment Dt)
