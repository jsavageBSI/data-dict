Description:
This is the accumulation of all the addon amounts that are considered in
a pending status, versus Work In Process (WIP). The value add
transaction totals are reflected in the pending status up until a
completion activity is performed. A completion activity will move that
portion of the totals from the "pending" fields to the "WIP" fields
which reflects General Ledger updating that has occured in the WIP GL
Accounts. The total value of the finished product always reflects the
totals of both the pending amounts and the WIP amounts.
