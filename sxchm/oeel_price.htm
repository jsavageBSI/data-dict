oeel.price - Price
  
Label: Price
Type: decimal
Format: zzzzzz9.99999
Decimals: 5
Initial: 0
Extent: 0
Mandatory: no
Val Exp: ?
Val Msg: 
Help: 
Trigger: 



Description:
The price of the line item. This field is stored per special costing
unit if the product is specially costed. If not, the price is stored per
unit on the line item.
 
Content:
The price can be calculated from PDSC records, manually entered, or
overridden via F12 function key functionality.
